# SE_project

Problem Statement

Finding available and qualified home healthcare professionals in Algeria is difficult, especially by region or specialization.

Why this problem is interesting

It tackles a real need in Algerian society where many patients require home medical assistance but lack a simple way to find trusted professionals.

Who faces this problem

Elderly people, patients with limited mobility, and families looking for at-home medical services.

Why it is important to solve the problem

Improving access to home healthcare can save time, reduce hospital congestion, and provide comfort for patients in need.

Why your solution to the problem would be the best

Our platform offers an organized, region-based directory with dynamic profiles and user-friendly search features, making it simple for users to find and contact healthcare providers quickly.

Product & Functionalities
Product Overview

MedHome DZ is a web-based platform that allows users to search for doctors, nurses, and caregivers who offer home services across Algeria’s 58 Wilayas. Medical professionals can register, create profiles, and display their experiences dynamically on the site.

Primary Functionalities

● Search healthcare providers by Wilaya and specialty
● Doctor/Nurse registration with profile creation (CV, experience, contact info)
● Dynamic display of newly added professionals on the homepage
● Responsive design for all devices (mobile, tablet, desktop)
● Database integration (MySQL) to store user and profile data

Wish List Features (Future Enhancements)

● User accounts for seekers to rate or favorite providers
● Map integration to visualize nearby doctors or nurses
● Appointment booking system
● Notification or email confirmation system for new registrations
